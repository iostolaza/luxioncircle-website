Exclusive Experiences

Hanbok Experience: Walk Through the Time of Palaces

When you step into a palace wearing our special concierge-curated hanbok, you won't just feel like a tourist, but a noble from the Joseon Dynasty. Capture the perfect photo under a traditional tiled roof and feel the history of the royal family all around you.

K-Beauty Shopping: A Journey to Discover Your Beauty

Find your true self! A dedicated beauty creator will accurately diagnose your skin type and personal color. Based on this, you'll get perfect product recommendations, from skincare to makeup, and even learn customized makeup techniques just for you.

Jjimjilbang Experience: The Essence of K-Healing

At our exclusive jjimjilbang, you'll experience this relaxing service from a dedicated "seshin-sa" (scrubber). Then, unwind in various themed rooms like the jewel, salt, ochre, and ice rooms.

Night View: Conquering the Night in Seoul

Forget ordinary night view tours. Our private guide will take you to hidden night spots that even Koreans don't know about, sharing beautiful stories along the way. Take in the glittering city from a private vantage point and create an unforgettable memory.

Temple Stay: Finding Serenity

Escape the bustling city and find peace of mind at a tranquil temple. In addition to meditation and conversations with a monk, this is a perfect culinary experience, especially for vegetarians, as you'll learn about and taste healthy temple cuisine.

Traditional Alcohol Experience: Crafting the Taste of Korea

We invite you to a private class with a master distiller, the owner of one of Korea's most popular traditional alcohol brands. This is more than just a class; it's an art form. Under expert guidance, you can even make a custom batch of makgeolli (rice wine) tailored to your personal taste.
